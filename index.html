Overview:

Our streaming podcast focuses on discussing the impact of social media on students. We delved into various aspects of this topic,
exploring how social media affects students' lives, behaviors, and social relationships. Additionally, we presented feedback
from our peers, ONL-Passion-10-1, sharing their perspectives on the influence of social media. We also had an engaging
discussion and insightful analysis with our audience, aiming to show the relationship between
students and social media in today's digital age.
